Jindal Panther Loyalty Program - FAQs


Nirman- Jindal Panther is a rewards program designed for influencers of Jindal Steel and Power products. The program aims to recognize and reward the loyalty of Masons/Contractors/Architects etc who consistently promote Jindal Steel and Power products. Participants of the program will earn points through their purchases and other engagement activities, which can then be redeemed for exclusive rewards and benefits. The program will provide dealers with personalized experiences, offers and benefits, based on their purchase history and level of engagement with the brand. The program aims to strengthen the relationship between Jindal Steel and Power and its influencers, fostering loyalty and long-term partnerships’.

Any Contractor/Mason/Architect/Engineer can join the program who is above 18 years old, is a resident of India and purchasing/influencing purchase of JSP Jindal Panther TMT Rebars from authorized JSP dealers.

JSP team will contact you wherein the entire Nirman program will be explained to you. After understanding the program, you may share your details to get enrolled in the program. You may also contact your nearest JSP dealer who will help you get in touch with JSP team for enrolment.

    Absolutely yes. As soon as threshold level of current tier is crossed, from next month onwards the tier is changed to applicable tier. For e.g. If in the 1st month of enrollment itself, a contractor’s site purchases 7 tons of Jindal Panther TMT Rebars. From next month he will be in silver tier.

    Based upon the tier in which an influencer currently is, he gets bonus points in addition to the base points earned by him during a month. These are called tier multiplier points. These are calculated by multiplying tier multiplier with base points. Tier multiplier is 75% for Platinum Tier, 50% for Gold Tier, 25% for Silver Tier.

    Accidental insurance is given to all influencers who are in bronze, silver, gold and platinum tier but the insurance cover varies from tier to tier. Health Insurance is given to influencers in Gold and Platinum Tiers only.

    Milestone gifts are those gifts which an influencer receives when he reaches a particular milestone of points. Point to be noted is that these gifts/benefits are given to influencer without deducting even a single point.
