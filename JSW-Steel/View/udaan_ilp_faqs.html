MSP UDAAN CONTRACTOR LOYALTY PROGRAM - FAQs

MSP Udaan Contractor Loyalty Program is a Loyalty Program from MSP where contractor partners can earn points against purchases of TMT Bars made from authorised MSP dealers and redeem their points against any gifts of their choice.

Only Contractor partners who are above 18 years old and a contractor profession and a resident of India purchasing MSP TMT Bars from authorized MSP dealers selling MSP TMT Bars.

  • Registration can be done by downloading the MSP Udaan loyalty program app and following the steps to fill mandatory details (Name, age, address, Aadhar no, dealer name, mobile no. etc) in order to register.
  • It is recommended that registrations be done by contacting the MSP Technical Service officers in your area and filling in all statutory details as required.
  • Call us on the HELPLINE for more details.

  • You can earn points by purchasing MSP TMT BARS from authorised MSP dealers.
  • For every one tonne of MSP TMT Bars procured, you earn 10 loyalty points. For smaller orders, you can earn points proportional to your order volume.
  • Program tiers are based on your qualifying purchases and actions in a rolling 365-day period.
  • For 365-days period after which you join the Program and/or enter a new Tier, you will earn Points from your qualifying purchases and actions based on the Multiplier benefit rate for that Tier.
  • Your Tier level for the subsequent annual period will be based on Transactions made from your qualifying purchases and actions, starting the first day after your enrolment and/or entry into a new Tier and ending on or around Day 365.
  • On Day 366, you may be placed in a different Tier depending on the volume of transactions you made in the immediately preceding 365-day period.
  • Tier upgrade - As and when the actual purchase points earned by the contactor partners reaches the tier milestone (refer to the tier design) in the last 365 days,the tier is auto-upgraded.
  • Tier dwelling rule - Continue enjoying your tier benefits to the fullest without any interruption. All you have to do is retain 75% of the point earnings you gather from the date of hitting a tier milestone until the next exit dateâ€”12 months from the date of enrolment.
  • Tier downgrade - Failure to retain a minimum 75% of the purchase points on a yearly cycle basis will result in automatic tier downgrade. Any downgrade will be effective after 12 months from the date of the last upgrade.
